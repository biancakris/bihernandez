It combines elements of design andn prototyping-in-stages, in an effort to combine advantages of top-down and bottom-up concepts. This method of development combines the features of the prototyping model and the waterfall model. Spiral model is favored for large, expensive, and complicated projects.

Advantages
Estimates(budget, schedule, etc.) become more realistic as work progressed, because important issues are discovered earlier. Early involvement of developers. Manages risks and develops the system into phases.

Disadvantages
High cost and time to reach the final product. Needs special skills to evaluate the risks and assumptions. Highly customized limiting reusability
