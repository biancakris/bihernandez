SDLC
Software Development Life Cycle(SDLC) is a process used for software projects. It is often used to design, develop, and test high quality sofwares. It is mainly used in the industry to ensure production of a high quality system that meets or exceeds customer expectations, attain completion within time and cost estimmates, effectively and efficiently works in the current and planned infrastructure, and is inexpensive to maintain well it being cost-effetive to enhance.
